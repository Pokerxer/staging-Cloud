    APS2 Customer Guide

    concepts
    Learn how your customers will work with Weebly and APS2

    Now that you’ve installed and configured APS plug-in, here’s how your customers will use the Odin Customer Control Panel (CCP) to purchase, view, and maintain their Weebly websites and subscriptions.

    Customer Control Panel

    Your customers manage their Weebly plans from the Home page of the CCP. First by selecting the subscription at the top right of the screen, then clicking the Subscription Resources link in the Service Information section of the page, they can view all current Weebly plans and subscriptions.

    Adding Weebly Service Plans

    Weebly plans are available to your users as resources. They can add a plan by clicking “Buy Additional Resources” on the landing page of their Customer Portal. Through this interface they can add units that represent the Weebly plans you offer (ex. Starter, Pro + Business).

    Creating New Sites

    Customers create and manage sites from the Weebly tab of the CCP.​ They create a site by clicking the Create button under “Site List.”

    Managing Sites

    Once a site is created, it’s listed on the Sites tab. From this tab the user can access the Weebly Site Editor by clicking the Edit My Site link. They can also optionally upgrade the plan offered on a site (if one is available).

    APS Upgrades

    One of the big advantages to using our APS2 module is the tight integration in the event a user upgrades while using the Weebly editor. The editor determines if a user has access to a given feature by the Weebly plan assigned to the site. If they try to use a feature not offered by their current plan they are prompted to upgrade. The screenshot below shows a user trying to access the HD Video element when they don’t have access to it.

    The modal window below displays when an upgrade is required to use a certain feature. They can opt to “Try it out” without upgrading; however, it will not display on the published site.

    When they click Upgrade Now an upgrade window is opened. This is known as the upgrade_url. You can learn more about upgrades here. The upgrade url should be set to the full url of your APS customer portal. This can be set in the Settings tab of Cloud Admin.

    Cancellations

    Customers can delete a site and cancel their plan from the CCP by clicking the Delete button on the Site Details page (accessed by clicking the site link from the Sites page).

    Deleting a site frees up that site’s plan for the customer to use on another site.
